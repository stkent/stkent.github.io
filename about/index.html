About Me

Hello! I'm a software developer with 3.5 years' mobile experience. I currently work at Detroit Labs creating native Android and iOS apps for national and international brands in the utilities, QSR, automotive, and pro audio industries. I write about all things software.

Before becoming a developer I earned my Ph.D. in Applied Math from the University of Arizona and taught several undergraduate classes.

If you'd like to know more about my background and specific experiences, please get in touch.
