About Me

Hello! I'm a software developer with 6 years of experience. I currently work at Detroit Labs as a Lead Developer creating native Android and iOS apps. I primarily write about mobile development, and occasionally explore the broader world of software development. The opinions expressed here are my own.

Before becoming a developer I earned my Ph.D. in Applied Math from The University of Arizona.

Other places you can find me on the internet include:

  • Twitter (DMs open for questions)
  • GitHub
  • StackOverflow

If you'd like to learn more about me, please get in touch.
